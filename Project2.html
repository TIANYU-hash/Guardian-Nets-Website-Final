The Climate Note

Project dates: 2025/01/15 – present

Overview

The Climate Note is Guardian Nets' environmental journalism platform. We publish student-written articles, research summaries, and interviews with environmental scientists and activists. Our goal is to make climate science accessible and highlight solutions-based stories. Articles are published bi-weekly and shared through our website and social media channels.

Goals

  • Publish 50 articles by end of 2025
  • Reach 5,000 monthly readers
  • Feature at least 10 student researchers per semester
  • Develop partnerships with environmental science departments
