Guardian Nets

Project dates: 2024/09/13 – present

Overview

Building on the original 8th grade shark tank project, Guardian Nets develops biodegradable fishing nets that decompose safely in marine environments. The project aims to reduce ghost nets—abandoned fishing gear that continues to trap marine life. Current focus is on material science research and small-scale field testing in collaboration with local fishermen.

Goals

  • Develop a fully biodegradable net material that maintains durability for 6-12 months
  • Reduce marine plastic pollution from fishing gear by 40% in test areas
  • Create affordable alternative to traditional nylon nets
  • Partner with 5 local fishing communities by end of 2025
