PFAs Literature Review

Project dates: 2025/03/01 – present

Overview

This project conducts a comprehensive literature review on PFAs (per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances), also known as "forever chemicals." The team is analyzing peer-reviewed studies on PFAs prevalence in water systems, health impacts, and current remediation technologies. The goal is to synthesize findings into accessible summaries for policymakers and the public.

Goals

  • Review 100+ academic papers on PFAs environmental impact
  • Create database of PFAs contamination hotspots globally
  • Summarize health risks for different exposure levels
  • Publish review paper in undergraduate research journal
  • Develop public guide to avoiding PFAs exposure
