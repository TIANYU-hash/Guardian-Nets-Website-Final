Middle School Workshop

Project dates: 2024/10/01 – 2025/05/30

Overview

The Middle School Afterschool Environmental Science Workshop was a 8-week program designed to introduce middle school students to environmental science concepts through hands-on activities. Topics included water quality testing, recycling, local ecology, and climate change basics. The program culminated in a student-led community project where participants designed posters and presentations for their school.

Goals

  • Engage 30+ middle school students in environmental science
  • Develop age-appropriate curriculum with 8 hands-on modules
  • Increase environmental literacy by 50% (pre/post survey)
  • Train 10 high school volunteers as teaching assistants
  • Create sustainable model for future workshops
