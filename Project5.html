Urban Reforestation

Project dates: 2025/02/01 – present

Overview

The Urban Reforestation Initiative aims to combat the urban heat island effect and improve air quality by planting native trees in underserved urban neighborhoods. Working with city parks departments and community organizations, we identify areas with low tree canopy coverage and organize volunteer planting events. The project also includes long-term tree maintenance and community education about the benefits of urban forestry.

Goals

  • Plant 500 native trees in urban areas by end of 2026
  • Increase tree canopy coverage by 15% in target neighborhoods
  • Engage 200+ community volunteers in planting events
  • Develop maintenance program with local partners
  • Create educational materials about urban forestry benefits
