Our Mission

Guardian Nets is a student organization/club dedicated to projects focusing on sustainability, environmental science and engineering.

Started from an 8th grade shark project on biodegradable fishing nets to address the issue of ghost nets, more students have gathered in high school as a club to brainstorm and create solutions on environmental issues that we care about.

From scientific research to journalism, Guardian Nets aims to take a step forward on the environmental issues that we care about. Our mission is to bridge the gap between awareness and action, providing practical solutions for a sustainable future.

Our journey so far

Since our founding in 2025, we've achieved significant milestones in our mission to create a greener future.

March 2025

Launched Article Series

We initiated our first comprehensive article series, covering topics from sustainable living to environmental policy analysis, reaching over 10,000 readers in the first month.

June 2025

Community Outreach Events

Organized our first community clean-up drives and educational workshops, engaging with local communities to promote hands-on environmental conservation.

September 2025

Strategic Partnerships Formed

Established partnerships with 5 local environmental organizations and 3 corporate sponsors committed to sustainable practices, amplifying our reach and impact.

December 2025

Digital Platform Expansion

Launched our interactive digital platform, increasing article engagement by 150% and establishing a growing online community of environmental advocates.
